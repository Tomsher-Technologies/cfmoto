    Design

    The 800NK's appearance takes inspiration from the V.02 concept design, featuring a fresh family language that imbues the NK series with a more youthful attitude. Its sharp lines, paired with a muscular fuel tank exude an air of boldness. The bike's lightweight design, tipping the scales at just 186KG, further enhances the 800NK's sporty attributes.

    • Handling

    Thanks to the excellent coordination of the electronic throttle, the 800NK can easily switch between three riding modes.The bike is equipped with adjustable damping KYB suspension, which maximizes the sporty style. The addition of steering damper and MAXXIS high-performance tires enhances both speed and confidence.Additionally, the bike is equipped with electronic systems such as ABS, CF-SC slipper clutch, and cruise control, ensuring a safe and enjoyable riding experience.
